About Me

I am a full stack developer that has recently made the career shift from the service industry. After 20 years of opening and running different bars and restaurants across Connecticut I wanted to look for a new challenge. While I loved the frenetic pace and ever-changing nature of my former career, it was beginning to leave me feeling unfulfilled. Learning these new skills seemed daunting, but I missed the thrill of learning something new and the satisfaction that comes with it.
